About Me

Hi, I'm Shreya V, an Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning undergraduate at Sharnabasva University, Kalaburagi, currently in my last year (Batch 2023–2027). My interest in tech started with wanting to understand how machines could learn to think and predict, and that curiosity has since grown into a passion for building complete, functional systems — not just models on paper, but real applications people can actually use.

Over the course of my degree, I've worked on projects that sit right at the intersection of AI and web development. My Multimodal Stress Detection system explores how combining different signals — rather than relying on just one — can lead to more accurate, human-centered predictions. With ArogyaAI, I focused on using AI to make healthcare support more accessible. And through my Integrated College Management Portal, I got to step outside pure ML and build a full-stack solution that solves a very real, everyday problem for students and administrators alike.
